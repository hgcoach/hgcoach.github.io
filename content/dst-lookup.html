When is DST
You can copy and paste the client's time zone from the "Time Zone" section in the client profile.

The "from" date is the time and time zone right before the time change, and the "to" date is right after.

The green dates are shown in the selected time zone, and the grey dates are shown in your system (or default) time zone.

Make sure you have selected the locale's true year-round time zone and not one of its semi-annual time zones.
