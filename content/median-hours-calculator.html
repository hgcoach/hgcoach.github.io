Median hours calculator
The median is the midpoint of a sorted set of data.

Coaches receive an incentive for each threshold reached by their median weekly coaching hours during a quarter. These thresholds are 13, 17, 20, and 24 hours.

There may be a small discrepancy between these numbers and the ones you see on your HG Dashboard. Neither calculation can yield a true median if the date range includes partial calendar weeks.
