Date and time format (preview)

A comma will separate the day of the week from the date if the day of the week is not 'none' and the date separator is 'space'.

A comma will separate the day of the month from the year if the date order is 'month day year' and the date separator is 'space'.
A 'the' will precede the day of the month if the day of the week is not 'none', the date order is 'day month year', the date separator is 'space', and the day format is 'ordinal'.
An 'of' will follow the day of the month if the day of the week is not 'none', the date order is 'day month year', the date separator is 'space', and the day format is 'ordinal'.


An 'at' will appear between the date and time.
The meridiem indicator will follow the time when using 12-hour clock format.
