Time zone comparison
This tool will show you recent and upcoming session times for you and your client.

The anchor will determine whose clock time the session will be anchored to in the event of time changes. If the clock time is different than the previous session, it will be highlighted yellow.

You can copy and paste the client's time zone from the "Time Zone" section in the client profile.

You can find a full list of "TZ identifiers" here.

Make sure you have selected the coach locale's true year-round time zone and not one of its semi-annual time zones.
Make sure you have selected the client locale's true year-round time zone and not one of its semi-annual time zones.
